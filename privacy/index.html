Privacy policy.

This policy explains what GameFeed stores, what third-party websites receive, and how Premium payments will be handled.

Effective July 5, 2026

1. Who operates GameFeed

GameFeed is operated by Richard Triggiani. Privacy questions may be sent to richiet147258@gmail.com.

2. Information GameFeed stores locally

The desktop application stores settings on your Windows computer, including:

  • custom keyboard and mouse shortcut selections;
  • your ad preference and the time a sponsored placement was last shown; and
  • embedded-browser cookies, cache, local storage, and login sessions created by the content platforms you use.

This local browser information may include platform account identifiers and session cookies. GameFeed does not operate a server that receives your platform passwords, messages, likes, or viewing history.

3. Website and download hosting

The GameFeed website is hosted using GitHub Pages and downloads are hosted using GitHub Releases. GitHub may process standard technical information such as your IP address, browser type, request time, and requested URL under GitHub’s privacy statement.

4. Third-party content platforms

TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, and Facebook load inside embedded browser views. These platforms may receive your IP address, cookies, browser or device information, account activity, searches, likes, and viewing behavior just as they would in a normal browser. Their privacy policies—not this policy—govern that processing.

GameFeed enables JavaScript, browser storage, and third-party cookies where needed for platform compatibility. Platforms may request camera or microphone access; GameFeed does not itself record or upload camera or microphone content.

5. Sponsored content

The Free plan may load a sponsored page from gamefeed.site after a natural video boundary and no more than once every 30 minutes of active use. If you intentionally open a sponsor’s link, that sponsor or advertising provider may receive your IP address, browser information, cookies, and click or referral information under its own privacy policy.

6. Premium payments

Premium checkout and recurring billing will be handled by Paddle as merchant of record. Paddle may collect your name, email, billing address, payment information, tax information, device information, and transaction history. GameFeed will receive only the customer, subscription, transaction, and entitlement information needed to provide Premium access. GameFeed will not receive or store full payment-card details. Paddle’s processing is governed by Paddle’s privacy notice.

7. Retention, choices, and rights

You can clear platform browser data by removing GameFeed’s local WebView profile or uninstalling the application and deleting its remaining local data. You can request information about account or Premium records controlled by GameFeed, or request correction or deletion where applicable, by emailing richiet147258@gmail.com. Paddle and each content platform handle requests for data they independently control.

8. Security and children

We use reasonable technical measures but cannot guarantee absolute security. Do not use GameFeed if you are not old enough to consent to the relevant content platforms or payment service in your jurisdiction. GameFeed is not directed to children under 13.

9. Policy changes

We may update this policy as GameFeed changes. The effective date above identifies the current version. Material changes will be communicated where required by law.
