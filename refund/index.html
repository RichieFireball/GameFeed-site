Refund and cancellation policy.

How to cancel GameFeed Premium or request help with a charge.

Effective July 5, 2026

1. Canceling Premium

You may cancel GameFeed Premium at any time through the subscription-management link in your Paddle purchase or renewal email. Cancellation prevents future renewals and normally takes effect at the end of your current paid billing period. You retain Premium access until that period ends unless the subscription is refunded or terminated earlier.

2. Refund requests

Paddle is the merchant of record for GameFeed Premium and handles payment refunds. Unless required by applicable law, transactions are generally non-refundable. Consumers may have statutory withdrawal or refund rights, including certain rights within 14 days of an eligible first subscription payment.

Submitting a request does not guarantee approval. Eligibility can depend on applicable law, product use, timing, technical defects, and Paddle’s review.

3. Technical problems

If a persistent technical defect prevents you from receiving Premium benefits, email richiet147258@gmail.com with your purchase email, operating-system version, and a description of the problem. Do not send passwords or complete payment-card information. We will attempt to resolve the issue and, where appropriate, support a refund request.

4. How to request a refund

Use the “Manage subscription” or receipt link in your Paddle email, or visit Paddle Buyer Support. Paddle’s current Refund Policy and Buyer Terms govern transactions processed by Paddle. Mandatory consumer rights are not limited by this policy.
